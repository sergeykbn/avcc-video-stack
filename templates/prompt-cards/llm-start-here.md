# LLM Start Here

Paste this into an LLM after sharing or linking this repo.

```text
I am trying to build a storyboard-style AI video.

Please use the workflow in this repo.

First, ask me what kind of video I want:
- Product video
- SaaS video
- Character/story video
- Fun cinematic video

Then ask for the references we need.

Before you begin, ask:
Do you want approval at every step, or should I use autopilot and only stop if something is risky or unclear?

Follow this process:
1. Creative brief
2. References
3. Shot list
4. Image prompts
5. Storyboard frames
6. Video prompts
7. Transition videos
8. Stitching
9. Final output

At each step, show me the result and ask whether to approve or revise.

Use attempts/, approved/, and disapproved/ folders.
Do not move to the next stage until the current stage is approved, unless I chose autopilot.
```
