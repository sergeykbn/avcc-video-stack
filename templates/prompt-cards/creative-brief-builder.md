# Creative Brief Builder

```text
Build a concise creative brief for this AI video.

Ask me only the questions that are missing:
- What is the video about?
- Who is watching it?
- What is the emotional arc?
- What references do we need?
- What must stay consistent?
- What should never appear?

Then produce:
- One-sentence premise
- Visual world
- Main character or product
- Required references
- Shot count
- Continuity locks
- Approval question
```
