# Video Prompt Builder

```text
Use the approved first frame and last frame as anchors.

Write a video prompt for each shot.

For each shot include:
- Duration
- First-frame anchor
- Last-frame anchor
- Camera movement
- Subject movement
- Timing beats
- What must stay consistent
- What must not happen

The model's job is to interpolate between the bookends.

End by asking:
Approve these video prompts, or revise?
```
