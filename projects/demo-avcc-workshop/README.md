# Demo — AVCC Workshop Reel

This is a **clickable example** of the full workflow. A real 30-second 9:16 ad for the AVCC online workshop, built with the same folder system you'll use for your own video.

Open the folders in order:

| Step | Folder | What's inside |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 1 | `02-references/` | Hero photo of the talent + b-roll screenshot references |
| 2 | `03-shot-list/storyboard.md` | The locked storyboard, shot by shot |
| 3 | `05-storyboard-frames/` | The four hero frames generated as anchors (cockpit, drift, fire, podium) |
| 4 | `06-video-prompts/seedance-prompt.md` | The final Seedance 2.0 prompt — written as two synced 15-second multi-shot generations with lip-sync VO |
| 5 | `07-transition-videos/` | The two Seedance renders (`v5-part1.mp4` + `v5-part2.mp4`) |
| 6 | `08-stitching/asset-ids.md` | Stitching notes and Higgsfield asset IDs |
| 7 | `09-final-output/avcc-workshop-final-30s.mp4` | The finished ad |

## What this demo shows

- **Real-photo image reference** for the on-camera talent (Rule 2 of the skill)
- **Two 15-second multi-shot Seedance generations** stitched together (option A from the skill's Rule 1 gate)
- **VO word distribution** balanced across both gens for matching speech speed (Rule 3)
- **AVCC visual density** — single palette, hero subject, dense cuts, macro cutaways, camera motion in every shot
- The whole journey: brief → frames → prompts → renders → stitch → final

## How to use it

Watch the final video first (`09-final-output/avcc-workshop-final-30s.mp4`), then walk backwards through the folders to see how each piece was built. When you make your own video, the structure of `projects/my-first-video/` mirrors this one exactly.
